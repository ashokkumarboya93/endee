About Errorlens AI

Errorlens AI is a professional AI debugging assistant that generates clean, structured debugging reports from user errors. It combines semantic vector search with Retrieval-Augmented Generation to provide accurate, context-aware solutions.

Simply paste your error message, and Errorlens AI will search through its database of 726+ known error patterns using Endee's vector search, find the most similar patterns, and generate a comprehensive report.
