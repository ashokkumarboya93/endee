Semantic Search

Unlike traditional keyword-based search, our system understands the meaning behind your error messages. When you paste IndexError: list index out of range, Errorlens AI doesn't just look for those exact words — it finds semantically similar patterns like off-by-one errors, boundary violations, and array access issues across all indexed languages.

The all-MiniLM-L6-v2 model converts text into 384-dimensional vectors, where similar meanings cluster together in vector space. The Endee Vector Database then performs lightning-fast cosine similarity search to find the most relevant matches.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

RAG is the technique that makes Errorlens AI truly intelligent. Instead of relying solely on a pre-trained LLM, we augment the AI's knowledge by first retrieving relevant error patterns from our curated database, then feeding that context into Google Gemini 2.0 Flash.

This approach ensures the AI response is grounded in real, verified error-solution pairs rather than hallucinated answers. The result: accurate root cause analysis, working code fixes, and actionable prevention tips — every single time.
